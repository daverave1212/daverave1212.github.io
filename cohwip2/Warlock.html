Warlock

Warlocks are people who made pacts with powerful beings.


Character Setup


Stats and Saves

Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1:

  • Charisma
  • Intelligence
  • Wisdom
  • Dexterity
  • Fortitude

As a Warlock, you have the following:

  • +2 Charisma Saves
  • +2 Fortitude Saves

You also have:

  • 1 Religion
  • 1 Knowledge
  • 1 Perception
  • -1 Stealth
  • -1 Luck
  • -1 Survival

Your starting Health is 9.

Your total Health will be 9 + Race Health + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 1).


When Leveling Up...

Whenever you gain a Level after Level 1, you gain:

  • +5 Health
  • +1 Skill Point
  • +1 extra known Spell or Maneuver

Other Things

If your Intelligence is at least 2, choose one Higher language you can speak.


When creating your character, choose one type of being you serve. That being is called your Patron: - Fey Patron, a wild, outworldly being, heavily magical or in tune with nature, and often outward strange. A Fey Patron often represents an old god of nature or magic, resembled by a magical animal or humanoid. - Celestial Patron, a being from a divine plain. Portrayed as angels or divine spirits of life and death - Demon or Devil patron, a being residing in Hell, an archdevil or lord in hell - Old God Patron, an eldritch being of nightmare and terror, whose thoughts, form and wants are incomprehensible to most people

Spells and Abilities

Choose between Intelligence and Charisma. This is your Main Stat.

Spell DC is 10 + Main Stat.

You start with 2 Charges.

You regain 1 Charge back after every worthy Combat encounter.

You can change your known Spells (not Talents) when taking a long rest.

Number of known maneuvers: 1 + Your Intelligence

Number of known spells: 2 + Your Intelligence + Your Level

Starting Abilities

Life Tap (0 Actions)

6 Health

Gain 1 Charge.


You can exceed 2 Charges with this. These Charges go away if you don't use them until the next Long Rest.



Specializations

  • Hexblade, a user of both magic and a weapon infused with the power of their master
  • Summoner, specialized in calling for aid from familiars and otherworldly beings

Hexblade

You start with the following Hexblade abilities:


Sentient Weapon (Special)

You have a Hexblade Weapon, inside which your patron lies, either possessing the weapon, being trapped inside it or using it as a means of communication with you.
Pick any weapon type, and it becomes your Hexblade Weapon.
You can customize your weapon's personality, voice, the way it looks. Talk to your DM about this.
You are automatically considered trained in your Hexblade Weapon.
There is no penalty to casting spells while wielding the Hexblade Weapon.

Hexblade (Passive)

After you cast an offensive spell, your next weapon attack deals +1d4 damage.


Offensive means that it deals damage or applies hard Crowd Control (anything better than Slow and creating Hard Terrain).


Talents

At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


Shapeshifting Weapon (0 Actions)

Once per turn

Instantly change the type of weapon of your Hexblade.

Hexstaff (Passive)

Your ~Hexblade~ Ability is changed and now has the following effect:
After you attack with a weapon, your next spell has +1 DC and deals +1d4 damage.

Doublehex (Passive)

You now have 1 more Hexblade Weapon, of any kind. The same patron or another one is possessing your second weapon.
At any time, you can vanish or resummon either weapon.
You must wait at least 5 minutes (for each weapon separately) after resummoning a weapon to vanish it again.


Summoner

You start with the following Summoner abilities:


Summon Familiar (1 Hour)

You conjure a ritual using various magical materials worth 25 Gold.
The first time you cast this ritual, you summon a familiar which becomes yours.
You can choose which type of familiar you want:
- It can take the shape of a small creature.
- It can look spectral or look as a normal animal with a slight glow (does not radiate light).
- You can choose what color it is if it's spectral or what color its glow is.
Also, choose an element which is bound to it: Fire, Water, Frost, Arcane, Thunder, Earth, Wind, Light or Darkness.
That becomes your Familiar Element.
Your familiar comes with a name. Roll for your familiar name on the Familiar Name Table (or your DM will choose one for you).
You can communicate with your Familiar telepathically.
The familiar doesn't take turns on its own, but at the same time as you.
On your turn, your familiar can use its own movement to move, but you must spend your actions for it to take actions.
If your familiar goes more than 20 meters away from you, it becomes unsummoned.
If it dies, it becomes unsummoned.
You can always summon it back with this Ability.
After your familiar is unsummoned, it needs 24 hours to be summonable back.
Your familiar has the following stats:
- Health: 8 + Your Fortitude + 2 * Your Level.
- Defense: Your Dexterity (minimum 0)
- Stats and Saves: Same as yours
- Movement: Same as yours, can't fly, but it can swim at half normal speed

Familiar Pet Attack (0.5 Actions)

1 turn

The pet melee attacks a target.
The attack deals 3d4 + 1 damage (of your pet's elemental type).


The attack is against target's Defense.

Element Lash (1 Action)

3 meters (from your Familiar)

The closest enemy (you choose if tie) to the familiar makes a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it takes 4d4 + 1 damage (of your Familiar Element), or half of that if it succeeds.

Soul Attack (1 Action)

1 Charge.

1 meter (from your Familiar)

Your familiar attacks a creature's soul and briefly disappears.
It appears back near you at the start of your next-next turn and deals 3d8 + 2 Dark damage (against Defense) but ignores half of the target's Defense.

Meat Shield (Reaction)

Long Rest

Passively, your pet doubles its maximum health and its size becomes Large.
When you are attacked, before the attack roll, you can activate this to make your pet dash to your position and the attack will be redirected to your pet.


Your pet must be within 6 meters of you.

Seduce (0.5 Actions)

1 Charge

6 meters (from your Familiar)

Long Rest

Your familiar tries to Charm an enemy (Wisdom save).
If it succeeds, your familiar also becomes Stunned.


Nothing happens if the target is immune to being Charmed.

Health Funnel (0 Actions)

Deal 6 damage to your Familiar, and you gain 4 Health.
Or..
Deal 6 damage to yourself, and your Familiar gains 4 Health.

Camouflage Master (1 Action)

1 Charge.

You and your familiar gain +5 Stealth for 10 minutes.

Chain Devour Magic (0.5 Actions)

1 Charge

12 meters

Cast one of these two effects:
1. Cure a crowd control, debuff, curse or poison on an ally.
2. Your pet tries to Silence a target (Intelligence save).
If it succeeds, it may try again on another target and the try gains +1 more to the DC than the previous try.


This can happen again and again until a target succeeds.

Sentience (Passive)

Your familiar can speak and communicate in all languages you know.
Your Spell DC also increases by 1 and your Maximum Health by 3.


Talents

At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


Tongues (Passive)

You can speak all Common languages.

Impmaster (Special)

When you get this Talent, gain 2 Skill Points.

Dream Walk (Channeled)

1 Charge

6 meters

Choose a sleeping target you can see.
It makes a Wisdom save with Disadvantage.
If it fails, you can alter their dream to your liking.
The target wakes up if they die in the dream, or the DM might have the target make another save for waking up later.
