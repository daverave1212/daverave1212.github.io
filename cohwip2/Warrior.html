Warrior

Warriors are highly trained combat experts


Character Setup


Stats and Saves

Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1:

  • Charisma
  • Wisdom
  • Dexterity
  • Fortitude

As a Warrior, you have the following:

  • +2 Dexterity Saves
  • +2 Fortitude Saves

You also have:

  • 1 Athletics
  • 1 Acrobatics
  • 1 Survival
  • -1 Investigation
  • -1 Deception
  • -1 Luck

Your starting Health is 10.

Your total Health will be 10 + Race Health + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 1).


When Leveling Up...

Whenever you gain a Level after Level 1, you gain:

  • +5 Health
  • +1 Skill Point
  • +1 extra known Spell or Maneuver

Other Things

A true warrior speaks only the language of battle! (Being a warrior does not grant you other languages, except the ones you already speak.)


Spells and Abilities

Choose between Charisma or Wisdom. This is your Main Stat.

Spell DC is 10 + Main Stat.

You start with 3 Charges.

You regain all Charges back when you Long Rest.

You can change your known Spells (not Talents) when taking a long rest inside a temple of your god.

Number of known maneuvers: 2 + Your Intelligence + Your Level


All Warrior Abilities are considered Maneuvers.

Starting Abilities

Advanced Flank (Passive)

When you flank-attack an enemy, or an ally flank-attacks an enemy with you, the flank damage bonus is +2/+4 (1-Handed/2-Handed).



Specializations

  • Fighter, a person specialized in weapon fighting and body-to-body combat
  • Battlemaster, a heavily trained and armed warrior; master yourself - master the enemy
  • Barbarian, a crude and battle thirsty warrior, who seeks to shed blood and lives for the fight
  • Marksman, a precise and mechanical soldier, always finding the best tactic and position; the Marksman wins the battle before it begins

Fighter

Lorem!


You start with the following Fighter abilities:


Overpower (0 Actions)

1 or 2 Charge(s)

Gain 0.5 Actions (or 1 Action) more this turn.
You can do this once per turn.


If you use 1 Charge, you gain 0.5 Actions. If you use 2 Charges, you gain 1 Action.

Upper Hand (Passive)

All attacks you make before you move in a turn count as if they are flanking.
If you are wielding a 1-Handed weapon, when you make an attack of opportunity, you can do 2 attacks of opportunity instead (with that weapon).
You have +2 in Athletics, Acrobatics and Survival.


Talents

At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


Master Positioner (Passive)

You permanently know all maneuvers.

Melee Pile (0 Actions)

1 Charge.

All Medium or smaller enemies within 2 meters of you are pulled to their closest points to you.


Battlemaster

You start with the following Battlemaster abilities:


Solid Defense (Passive)

Whenever you are attacked (after damage), your Defense is reduced by 1.
After every Long Rest, your Defense resets and is increased by +1 on top of it.

Defense Up (0.5 Actions)

1 Charge.

Increase your Defense by 2.

Obliterate (0 Actions)

1 Charge

Deal bonus damage on top of an attack equal to your current Defense.
Reset your Defense.


Talents

At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


Protect the Meek (Reaction)

Encounter

When an ally within 3 meters of you is targeted by a ranged attack, you become its target instead.

Master Positioner (Passive)

You permanently know all maneuvers.

Bashing Charge (1 Action)

1 Charge.

Move 3 meters in a straight line, then choose a target near you.
That target takes 1d8 damage and try to Stun the target (Fortitude save).


Barbarian

AAAAAAAAAAAA LOREMM!!


You start with the following Barbarian abilities:


Onslaught (Passive)

Whenever YOU kill a worthy enemy, heal for 1d6.

Reckless Attack (0 Actions)

1 Charge.

After you land an attack, deal 2d6 damage to you and also add that damage to the attack.

Undying Rage (Reaction)

1 Charge

Long Rest

Taking damage that would kill you leaves you at 1 Health instead.

Blood Boil (0 Actions)

1 Charge.

Heal for 2d6 health.


Talents

At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


Heartbeat Trance (0 Actions)

1 Charge.

Take 2d6 damage.
Enter a Heart Trance until the end of your next turn.
While in Heart Trance, you take 50% less damage from Fire, Frost, Necrotic and Thunder damage and you have +3 on Acrobatics and Intimidation rolls.
When Heart Trance ends, heal for 2d6.

Rage Trance (0 Actions)

1 Charge.

Take 2d6 damage.
Until the end of your next turn, you are immune to stuns, slows, snares, cripples and being knocked prone.
Your attacks while in Rage Trance have an extra +1 damage to all attacks and you have +3 on Athletics.
When Rage Trance ends, heal for 2d6.

Battle Trance (0 Actions)

1 Charge.

Take 2d6 damage.
Enter a Battle Trance that lasts until the end of your next turn.
You gain a protective shield around you which absorbs up to 4 + Your Level physical damage.
When Rage Trance ends, heal for 2d6, and the shield fades.


Marksman

You start with the following Marksman abilities:


Marksmanship (Passive)

YOU can flank a target from up to 6 meters away (if you, the target and the ally are on the same line).


As usual, that ally helping with your flank must right behind the target.

Reload (Passive)

Your first regular weapon attack in an encounter deals +1 damage.
Your second regular weapon attack in the encounter deals +3 damage.
Your third regular weapon attack and all upcoming ones in the encounter deal -5 damage.
You can spend 1 Charge to 'reset' this Ability to its original state.

Point Mark (0 Actions)

15 meters

Short Rest

Mark a point on the ground.
Allies and you can use the mark to flank targets.
Those attacks do benefit from Advanced Flank.

At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


Recoil Shot (0 Actions)

1 Charge

2 meters

Dash up to 2 meters away from the target.
Try to push the target 2 meters away (Fortitude save).


This does not trigger attacks of oportunity.

Harpoon (1 Action)

1 Charge

6 meters

Make a ranged attack that deals 2d12 + 4 damage (against Defense).
The target makes a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it is pulled to you.
Otherwise, you are pulled to it.


Requires a Harpoon, which can be bought for 50 Gold.

Precise Weapon (1 hour)

Choose any weapon you have and make it Precise.
The weapon's scaling becomes +3/+6 (1-Handed/2-Handed) instead of scaling with a Stat.
The wielder of the weapon is also immune to Cripple.


Making another weapon Precise makes the previous back to how it was. Requires to be at a forge.
