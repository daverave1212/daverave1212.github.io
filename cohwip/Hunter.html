Ranger

You are a hunter, tracker, always on your toes. No matter your foe, be it a beast, a vampire or a dragon, you know best how to deal with it.

When creating your character:

a) Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Fortitude, Dexterity, Wisdom

b) You have +3 in Dexterity Saves.

Skills

You have +1 Nature, +1 Stealth, +1 Perception

You have -1 Religion, -1 History, -1 Performance

Starting Stats:

Base Health: 7

Bonus Health when leveling up: +5

Languages

If your Intelligence is at least 2, choose one Common or Wild language you can speak.

Spellcasting and Main Stat

Choose between Wisdom or Charisma. This is your Main Stat.

Your Spell DC is 10 + (Main Stat).

Your Spell Hit Bonus is (Main Stat).

You are Trained in Spells.

Some spells will tell you to add your Power to the damage dice.

Your Race tells you what your Power is.

You have 3 Charges: O O O

Certain Abilities require you to spend one Charge.

You regain all Charges back when you Long Rest.

All Hunter-specific abilities are considered spells (unless stated otherwise).

Abilities

You start with the following class abilities:

Slay : 0 Actions

Spend 1 Charge.
Your next attack has the following effect:
If the target is below (or equal to) 50% Health, it does 1d12 bonus damage if it hits.
Otherwise, heal deal 1d6 damage to the target and to all creatures around the target if it hits.

Tumble : 0 Actions

Move 1 meter in any direction, without triggering attacks of oportunity.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Other Notices

When creating your character, choose one of these abilities:

Wicked Slayer : Passive

Your attacks against Monstrosities, Celestials and Unholy creatures have +1 to hit.
Your attacks are always treated as Magical or Silver for the purpose of some effects.

Mage Slayer : Reaction

Spend 1 Charge.
Use when an enemy casts a spell.
That enemy rolls a Wisdom save.
If it fails, the spell is negated.

Giant Slayer : Passive

Weapon attacks and thrown spells deal +1d4 damage against Large or larger targets.
Enemies don't gain flanking bonus when attacking you.

Mankind Slayer : Passive

Against Humanoids, your attacks crit on natural 19 rolls.
If a Slay attack crits, all the Slay bonus damage crits too (double it).

Casting Spells and Maneuvers

You know a total number of spells and maneuvers equal to 2 + Half of Your Level + Your Intelligence.

You can reasign your spells whenever you level up.

This is your spell list:

  • Maneuvers: All
  • Basic Spells:
  • Conjure Ammo
  • Detect Poison and Disease
  • Land Breach
  • Flameblade
  • Light
  • Purify Food and Drink
  • Normal Spells:
  • Chaining Grasp
  • Absorb Element
  • Alarm
  • Charming Word
  • Cure Poison
  • Fairy Fire
  • Fake Sound
  • Influence Beast
  • Barkskin
  • Sense Magic
  • Speak With Animals
  • Wild Heart
  • Higher Level Spells:
  • Magic Prison

Choosing Your Specialization...

At Level 1, you can choose your specialization.

Every Hunter can choose from the following Hunter specializations:

  • Draven, a tracker and hunter of beats and men, never lost and never without purpose
  • Warden, a potent user of fast and practical magic to use against foes, and with allies
  • Beastmaster, a pack hunter, who finds friends in even the most ruthless of beasts

Tracker : Passive ~~0.5 Chargess

o You can more easily track creatures.
When you try to track a creature, your roll for that try gains +3.
If you succeed, you may learn about its size, how many of them there are, if it's injured or roughly how far away it is.

Hunter's Mark : 0 Actions

Spend 1 Charge.
Choose and mark a target within 12 meters.
Your single target attacks and thrown spells against the marked target have the damage dice increased by 1 tier.
(Ex: instead of 2d6, an attack would be 2d8.)
(d12's don't increase, they stay d12.)
When the target with the mark dies, you can use your Reaction to freely move the mark to another target.
You can do this only once per Long Rest.

At Level 2, pick one of the following Talents and learn it permanently:

Animal Companion : Passive

You have an animal pet that can fight alongside you!
Your animal has stats depending on its species.
By default, apply the following stats to your pet (these will get modified by your pet's species):
- Health: Half of yours
- Movement: 6 meters
Your pet takes its turn at the same time as you do, and always has the same initiative as yours.
Your pet can make normal Skill Checks using your Skill bonuses, or its own, using its special Stats.
Your pet will not stray more than 30 meters away from you in hostile territory.
On your turn, you can command it to move, but it can't take its own Actions.
You and your pet share the same Actions on a turn.
(ex: If you have 1 Action, your pet can use 0.5 Actions to attack and you can use 0.5 Actions to attack too, or your pet spends 1 Action and you skip your Actions, etc.)
All pets have the Pet Attack ability:

Pet Attack : 0.5 Actions

The pet melee attacks a target.
The attack has + your Main Stat.
The attack deals 1d6 + your Main Stat - 1 (can't be lower than 1d6 - 1).
You can do this once per turn.
Some abilities may say 'Perform a Pet Attack'. In that case, ignore the once-per-turn.

Flare Shot : 0.5 Actions

Spend 1 Charge.
Shoot a flare at a target point within 15 meters.
The area around the flare lights up in a 6 meter radius, creating normal light.
When cast, all allies in the area are cleared of Stun, Fear or Cripple.
Allies in the area have Advantage on rolls against Fear.
The flare lasts 1 minute.

Night Senses : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge.
For 1 Hour, you gain the following:
Your party has Dark Vision (15 meter range).
Your party has Advantage on rolls against Blinding.
Your party has +2 meter movement speed.

At Level 4

Multishot : 1 Action

Explosive Shot : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge.
Perform a ranged attack.
If it hits, the target takes 2d10 damage, and all adjacent creatures must make a Dexterity save.
If they fail, they take half of the damage of the primary target.

Warden : Passive ~0.50

When creating your character, you can choose 2 more spells to know permanently from the Mage or Cleric spells list.
You also gain +1 in Arcana, Luck and Knowledge, and your Spell DC is increased by 2.
Slay is replaced by Quick Magic. Quick Magic is considered a Spell.

Quick Magic : 0 Actions ~0.25

Spend any number of Charges.
Choose one of the following effects, then apply the exact same effect for each Charge spent (on the same target):
- Instantly deal 1d6 Fire, Frost or Shock damage to a target, and permanently reduce its Armor by 1.
- Instantly heal a target for 1d6.
- Instantly dash 3 meters, ignoring attacks of oportunity, and going through enemies and small obstacles or medium gaps.
You can do this once per turn.
3 meters range for the damage effects.
Note: If you see Quick Magic +1, it means a Quick Magic worth 1 Charge, +2 means worth 2 Charges, etc.

After using Tumble, you can instantly cast Quick Magic +1 once without spending Charges.

Watcher Form : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge.
You take on a special form, called a Watcher Form.
Your body becomes engulfed in a magical aura (you choose its color), your eyes glow, and you can gain other aesthetics-only body transformations (customize it with your DM).
While in this form, your attacks have +2 to hit to all attacks, you gain +2 to all saves and +2 to your Save DC and +3 to Intimidation and Arcana.
Also, when you cast a spell (before you do), you can instantly dash 3 meters, ignoring attacks of oportunity, and going through enemies and small obstacles or medium gaps.
Lasts up to 1 minute.
Can be used only once per Long Rest.

Guard Breaker : Passive

Your Slash, Smash and Pierce attacks ignore target's resistances to that damage type (not immunity, however).

Quickfire : 0 Actions

Spend 1 Charge.
Perform an attack with a (loaded) 1-Handed ranged weapon.
This attack does not trigger attacks of oportunity and can be used in melee range without penalty.
