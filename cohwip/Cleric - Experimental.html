Cleric

Clerics are people attuned to a certain god.

When creating your character:

Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Charisma, Intelligence, Wisdom, Dexterity, Fortitude

Choose one: +1 Weapon Effect or +2 Spell DC

Choose one: +2 Fortitude Save or +2 Dexterity Save

Skills

You have +1 Religion, +1 Luck, +1 Insight

You have -1 Biology, -1 Investigation, -1 Deception

Starting Stats:

Base Health: 7 + (Fortitude x 2)

Bonus Health when leveling up: +5

Languages

If your Intelligence is at least 2, choose one Higher or Common language you can speak.

Spellcasting and Main Stat

Choose between Wisdom or Charisma. This is your Main Stat.

Your Spell DC is 8 + (Main Stat).

Your Spell Hit Bonus is (Main Stat).

You are Trained in Spells.

Some spells will tell you to add your Power to the damage dice.

Your Race tells you what your Power is.

You have 3 Charges: O O O

Certain Abilities require you to spend one Charge.

You regain all Charges back when you Long Rest.

Starting Abilities

Loyalty : Special

You have a special score called Loyalty.
You start with 2 Loyalty.
Special Cleric abilities influence your Loyalty once you get them.

Power of a God : 0 Actions

Invoke the power of your god.
Instantly heal or damage a target for an amount equal to your Loyalty.
3 meters range.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Other Notices

The first time you choose a cleric domain (specialization), you must choose between two abilities.

For example, for Battle Cleric, you have to choose either March Ahead or Piety.

Choose wisely...

Battle Cleric

Battle clerics specialize in conditions of war. You must choose your side

Chains : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge and choose a target within 6 meters.
The target is healed for 1d12.
Then, it makes a Wisdom Save.
If it fails, it is Paralyzed for 1 minute.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

March Ahead : 0 Actions

Loyalty : +1
This turn, you can move more meters equal to your Loyalty.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Piety : Passive

Loyalty : +3

Eternal Bindings : 0 Actions or Reaction

Loyalty: +1
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature within 3 meters makes a Wisdom save.
If it fails, it becomes magically bound to an object within 3 meters of it and can't willingly move more than 3 meters away from that object.
The creature can roll the save again at the start of their turn to escape the binding.
Lasts up to 1 minute.

Heavy Burden : 0 Actions

Loyalty: +2
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature within 3 meters makes a Wisdom save.
If it fails, it is Crippled.

Pain of War : 0.5 Actions

Loyalty: +1
Spend 1 Charge.
You gather divine energy to inflict heavy pain upon creatures in your surroundings.
All creatures within 3 meters of you take 4 True damage.
If a creature can't feel pain, it is unaffected.
Creatures affected can tell you did this...
