Bertle

When creating your character:

a) Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Wisdom, Intelligence, Dexterity or Charisma

b) You have +1 in Dexterity saves.

Skills

You have +1 Acrobatics, +1 Perception, +1 Craft

You have -1 Animal Handling, -1 Deception, -1 History

Starting Stats:

Base Health: 10

Movement: 7 meters

Lifespan: ~75 years

Languages: Common

Languages

If your Intelligence is at least 2, you can speak 1 additional Common language.

No case for tag Undefined

Abilities

You start with the following racial ability:

Herbivore : Passive

You can not eat meat.
If you do, you take take 1d12 Poison damage and have Disadvantage on all rolls for 1 hour.
You take 50% more damage from Poison (other than when eating meat).

Level Up Bonuses

At Level 1, you can pick one of the following talents and know it permanently:

Fleet Footed : Passive

You are immune to slows.

Nimbleness : Passive

You can move through the spaces of enemy units (in normal conditions).

Darkvision : Passive

You can see up to 12 meters away in darkness, without discerning colors.

Cutting Teeth : Passive

Your teeth act as natural daggers which can pierce through some materials:
You are able to cut rope, flesh, wood, etc (but not concrete, metal, etc).
Attacking with your teeth takes 1 Action, adds Dexterity modifier and deals 1d8 Piercing damage.
