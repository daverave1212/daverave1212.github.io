Orc

Tribal in nature, though not stupid. Orc societies are highly based on honor and strength. Many orcs are also in tune with nature or spirits, allowing them to be both powerful and cunning. Unlike orcs in traditional fantasy, Orcs in Call of Heroes are not necessarily vile and reckless. Orcs are leaders and hunters by nature, but will also be lead by someone stronger or who can indeed lead better than them. Even in a more peaceful tribe, the most charismatic, witty and strong orc will be the leader. Orcs are also driven by control (over territory, goods, etc). Many of them wish to be in control: they don't like other people doing things for them, unless they are sure they can do it better. For this reason, many orcs make a living without help. They build their own houses, hunt their own food or pillage for both the good of the tribe and their own.

When creating your character:

a) Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Fortitude, Wisdom, Dexterity or Charisma

b) You have +1 in Fortitude saves.

c) You start with 1 extra known maneuver.

Skills

You have +1 Athletics, +1 Perception, +1 Intimidation

You have -1 Knowledge, -1 Insight, -1 Persuasion

Starting Stats:

Base Health: 9

Movement: 6 meters

Lifespan: ~80 years

Languages: Common and Orcish

Weapon Skills and Language

If your Intelligence is at least 1, you can speak an additional Wild Language.

You are trained in Hand Axes and Battle Axes.

Abilities

You start with the following racial abilities:

Boiling Blood : 0 Actions

Lose half of your current health.
Your next attack gains Advantage on the Attack roll and it crits if it hits
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Battle Anguish : Passive

Whevener you drop to 0 Health, your maximum Health permanently decreases by 1.

Beast Rider : Passive

You are trained in Riding and Exotic Riding (you can ride exotic mounts, such as wolves).
You also have such an exotic mount of your choice, if the DM accepts it.
This mount is a non-combat pet. It does not participate in combat.
You gain +2 in Animal Handling.
