Warlock

Warlocks are people who made pacts with powerful beings

When creating your character:

a) Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Charisma, Intelligence, Wisdom, Dexterity, Fortitude

b) You have +2 in Fortitude Saves and Charisma Saves.

Skills

You have +1 Religion, +1 Knowledge and +1 Perception.

You have -1 Stealth, -1 Luck and -1 Survival

Starting Stats:

Base Health: 3

Bonus Health when leveling up: +6

Languages

If your Intelligence is at least 2, choose one Higher language you can speak.

Spellcasting and Main Stat

Choose between Intelligence or Charisma. This is your Main Stat.

Your Spell DC is 10 + (Main Stat).

Your Spell Hit Bonus is (Main Stat).

You are Trained in Spells.

Some spells will tell you to add your Spell Power to the damage dice.

Your Race tells you what your Spell Power is.

You have 2 Charges: O O

Certain Abilities require you to spend Charges.

You regain all Charges back when you rest.

All Warlock-specific abilities are considered spells.

Abilities

You start with the following class abilities:

Life Tap : 0.5 Actions

Take 6 damage.
Gain 1 Charge.

Other Notices

Choose one of the following types of patrons:

Fey Patron : Passive

Your lord is a higher fey creature.
You can pick a Racial Ability from any other race and have it permanently.
Your Spell DC increases by +2 when you try to put enemies to Sleep or to Charm them.

Celestial Patron : Passive

Your lord is a celestial being, who lives in a higher plane.
Your Spell DC increases by +2 when you try to Snare or Blind enemies.

Life Cycle : 0.5 Actions

Take 6 damage and heal an ally within 3 meters for 6 Health.
Or deal 6 damage to a willing ally within 3 meters and heal yourself for 6 Health.

Demon Pact : Passive

Your lord is a powerful demon.
Once per Long Rest, you can use Life Tap for 0 Actions and without taking Damage.
Your Spell DC increases by +2 when you try to Stun enemies.

Old God Pact : Passive

Your lord is an old, slumbering god.
The first time you choose a Race talent, gain all of them.

Now that your character is almost ready, choose your specialization:

Choosing Your Specialization...

At Level 1, you can choose your specialization.

Every Warlock can choose from the following Warlock specializations:

  • Hexblade, a user of both magic and a weapon infused with the power of their master
  • Summoner, specialized in calling for aid from familiars and other worldly beings
  • Waverer, whose master's magic is far beyond its control: one who should expect the unexpected

Sentient Weapon : Passive

You have a Hexblade Weapon, inside which your patron lies, either possessing the weapon, being trapped inside it or using it as a means of communication with you.

Summoner

Lorem ipsum snitel dolor.

Summon Familiar : 1 Hour

You conjure a ritual using various magical materials worth 25 Gold.
The first time you cast this ritual, you summon a familiar which becomes yours.
You can choose which type of familiar you want:
- It can take the shape of a small creature.
- It can look spectral or look as a normal animal with a slight glow (does not radiate light).
- You can choose what color it is if it's spectral or what color its glow is.
Also, choose an element which is bound to it: Fire, Water, Frost, Arcane, Thunder, Earth, Wind, Light or Darkness.
That becomes your Familiar Element.
Your familiar comes with a name. Roll for your familiar name on the Familiar Name Table (or your DM will choose one for you).
You can communicate with your Familiar telepathically.
The familiar doesn't take turns on its own, but at the same time as you.
On your turn, your familiar can use its own movement to move, but you must spend your actions for it to take actions.
If your familiar goes more than 20 meters away from you, it becomes unsummoned.
If it dies, it becomes unsummoned.
You can always summon it back with this Ability.
After your familiar is unsummoned, it needs 24 hours to be summonable back.

Your familiar has the following stats:

- Health: 10 + Your Fortitude + 2 per level after level 1.

- Armor: 10 + Your Dexterity

- Stats and Saves: Same as yours

- Movement: Same as yours, can't fly, but it can swim

- Attack Bonus: +3

Pet Attack : 0.5 Actions

The pet melee attacks a target.
The attack deals 1d8 + 1 damage (of your pet's elemental type).
You can do this once per turn.

As a summoner, you have access to special Familiar feats.

These feats give you extra abilities for your Familiar.

When creating your character, you also get one of these feats for free.

Element Lash : 1 Action

The closest creature (you choose if tie) to the familiar makes a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it takes 2d8 + 2 damage (of your Familiar Element).
3 meter range.

Soul Attack : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge.
Your familiar makes a melee attack against a creature.
If it hits, it deals 4d8 - 2 damage (of your Familiar Element).
After that, your familiar briefly disappears.
It appears back near you at the start of your next-next turn.

Meat Shield : Reaction

Passively, your pet doubles its maximum health and its size becomes Large.
When you are attacked, before the attack roll, you can activate this to make your pet dash to your position and the attack will be redirected to your pet.
1 use / Long Rest.

Sentience : Passive

Your familiar can speak and communicate in all languages you know.
Your Spell DC also increases by 1.

Seduce : 0.5 Actions

Spend 1 Charge and choose a target within 6 feet of the familiar.
The target rolls a Wisdom save. If it fails, it is Charmed by your familiar, and your familiar is also Charmed by that creature.
(Nothing happens if the target is immune to being Charmed).

Devour Magic : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge.
Cure a debuff or curse on an ally.
Also, choose a target near the familiar.
That target rolls an Intelligence save.
If it fails, it is Silenced and takes 1d10 damage (of your Familiar Element).

Health Funnel : 1 Action

Deal 6 damage to your Familiar, and you gain 3 Health.
Or..
Deal 6 damage to yourself, and your Familiar gains 3 Health.

Camouflage Master : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge.
You and your familiar gain +5 Stealth for 10 minutes.

At Level 2, pick one of the following Talents and learn it permanently:

Tongues : Passive

You can speak all common languages.

Drain Soul : 1 Action

Choose a target within 3 meters.
Deal 3 Necrotic damage to that target.
If it dies until the start of your next turn, you gain 1 Charge.

Dream Walk : Channeled

Spend 1 Charge.
Choose a sleeping target you can see within 6 meters.
It makes a Wisdom save with Disadvantage.
If it fails, you can alter their dream to your liking.
The target wakes up if they die, or the DM might have the target make another save for waking up later.
