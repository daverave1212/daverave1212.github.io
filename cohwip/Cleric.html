Cleric

Clerics are people attuned to a certain god.

When creating your character:

Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Charisma, Intelligence, Wisdom, Dexterity, Fortitude

Save Bonuses:

You have +2 in Wisdom Saves and Charisma Saves.

Skills

You have +1 Religion, +1 Luck, +1 Insight

You have -1 Biology, -1 Investigation, -1 Deception

Starting Stats:

Base Health: 7

Bonus Health when leveling up: +5

Languages

If your Intelligence is at least 2, choose one Higher or Common language you can speak.

Spellcasting and Main Stat

Choose between Wisdom or Charisma. This is your Main Stat.

Your Spell DC is 10 + (Main Stat).

Your Spell Hit Bonus is (Main Stat).

You are Trained in Spells.

Some spells will tell you to add your Power to the damage dice.

Your Race tells you what your Power is.

You have 3 Charges: O O O

Certain Abilities require you to spend one Charge.

You regain all Charges back when you Long Rest.

All Cleric-specific abilities are considered spells (unless stated otherwise).

Abilities

You start with the following class abilities:

Loyalty : Special

You have a special score called Loyalty.
You start with 2 Loyalty.
Special Cleric abilities influence your Loyalty once you get them.

Power of a God : 0 Actions

Invoke the power of your god.
Instantly heal or damage a target for an amount equal to your Loyalty.
3 meters range.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Casting Spells and Maneuvers

You know a total number of spells and maneuvers equal to 2 + Your Level + Your Intelligence.

You can reasign your spells whenever you level up.

Other Notices

The first time you choose a cleric domain (specialization), you must choose between two abilities.

For example, for Battle Cleric, you have to choose either March Ahead or Piety.

Choose wisely...

Choosing Your Specialization...

At Level 1, you can choose your specialization.

Every Cleric can choose from the following Cleric specializations:

  • Battle Cleric, where you become devoted to either Peace or War (or both) and pursue either of those
  • Beacon Cleric, a keeper of the light or darkness, who power draws from flame or void...
  • Life Cleric, a balancer between life and death; your very fate hangs in this balance

Battle Cleric

Battle clerics specialize in conditions of war. You must choose your side

You have the following ability:

Chains : 1 Action

Spend 1 Charge and choose a target within 6 meters.
The target is healed for 1d12.
Then, it makes a Wisdom Save.
If it fails, it is Paralyzed for 1 minute.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Choose your side, Battle Cleric!

Pick only one of the following two abilities!

March Ahead : 0 Actions

Loyalty : +1
This turn, you can move more meters equal to your Loyalty.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Piety : Passive

Loyalty : +3

At Level 2, pick one of the following Talents and learn it permanently:

Eternal Bindings : 0 Actions or Reaction

Loyalty: +1
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature within 3 meters makes a Wisdom save.
If it fails, it becomes magically bound to an object within 3 meters of it and can't willingly move more than 3 meters away from that object.
The creature can roll the save again at the start of their turn to escape the binding.
Lasts up to 1 minute.

Heavy Burden : 0 Actions

Loyalty: +2
Spend 1 Charge.
A creature within 3 meters makes a Wisdom save.
If it fails, it is Crippled.

Pain of War : 0.5 Actions

Loyalty: +1
Spend 1 Charge.
You gather divine energy to inflict heavy pain upon creatures in your surroundings.
All creatures within 3 meters of you take 4 True damage.
If a creature can't feel pain, it is unaffected.
Creatures affected can tell you did this...

Beacon Cleric

As a cleric, you become a devotee of darkness or/and light.

You have the following ability:

Grasp : 0.5 Actions

Pick a target within 6 meters (you don't need line of sight).
That target does not benefit from partial cover for 1 minute.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Choose your side, Beacon Cleric!

Pick only one of the following two abilities!

Radiance : Passive

Loyalty : +1
At all times, you radiate light in a 3 meter radius.
You can turn this on and off at will.

Twilight : Passive

Loyalty : +1
You have Dark Vision.
You are immune to being Blinded.

At Level 2, pick one of the following Talents and learn it permanently:

Let There Be Light : Passive

Loyalty: +1
The target of Grasp doesn't benefit from Total Cover, and takes 1d6 Fire damage.

Let There Be Darkness : Passive

Loyalty: +0
When using Power of a God on an enemy, it also rolls a Wisdom save.
If it fails, it is Blinded.
When using Power of a God on an ally, it gains Dark Vision for up to 10 minutes.

Mass Grasp : Passive

Loyalty: +2
Grasp now affects all enemies in a 4x4 area within 9 meters instead of 1 target creature.

Soul Cleric

You are a worshipper of life and/or death. Seek their balance, and seek balance within yourself.

You have the following ability:

Life/Death

Life Cycle : 0.5 Actions

Transfer 6 health from a willing living creature to another willing living creature.
Both creatures must be within 3 meters of you.

Choose your side, Life Cleric!

Pick only one of the following two abilities!

Keeper of Life : 0 Actions

Loyalty : +1
Choose an ally.
The next time it takes damage, you take that damage instead.

Keeper of Death : Passive

Loyalty : +1
Your attacks deal +1d6 damage against Undead once per turn.
You are immune to being Feared.

At Level 2, pick one of the following Talents and learn it permanently:

True Necromancy : 1 Action

Loyalty: +1
You have an Undead servant, a creature previously alive whose soul you are forcing to stay in this world against its will and do your bidding.
Come up with a story about why you have an Undead servant, and why your god allowed this or gave him or her to you.
_For example, your servant was in a group of cultists who killed your family. Now, your god gave you the power to resurrect one of these cultists to aid you in killing the rest of the cult._
Your servant must follow your orders as long as it is related to the reason you are forcing it to stay.
The servant must be a sentient humanoid and must be within 15 meters of you at all time, otherwise it will permanently lose 1 Maximum health for each hour spent apart.
It has the following stats:
Health: 1/3 of your maximum health
Armor: Same as yours, but -1
Stats: All 0, except one which is 2 (you choose which).
Movement: 5 meters
Initiative: Always equal to yours, and takes its turn before or after you (you choose every time).
It can use 1 Action to make a melee attack on its turn: +3 to hit, 1d850%.
