Exhaust-Based Spellcasting

Cleric Abilities are Exhaust-based.

As a Cleric, you know a certain number of Advanced Abilities. You can cast each Advanced Ability you know ONCE, then it becomes unusable (Exhausted) until your next Long Rest.
All your Abilities Un-exhaust when you finish a Long Rest. Advanced Abilities are all Abilities from the Spell Lists listed as Advanced.

Changing Spells

You can change your known Spells (not Talents) when taking a Long Rest.
Talents can't generally be changed once picked; they are permenant decisions.All Cleric-specific abilities are considered spells (unless stated otherwise).

Starting Abilities

Awe
0 Actions
5 meters
Instantly heal or damage a target for 1d4 (True damage). You can do this once per Long Rest. Some other Cleric spells will cast Awe for free.
Clerics start with the Awe Spell. Your future Cleric Talents will <i>trigger</i> Awe casts for free or reset its cooldown.

Life Cleric

You are a worshipper of life and/or death. Seek their balance, and seek balance within yourself.\nYou have a Second Soul character, which is essentially another toned-down character you play.

You start with...

Second Soul
Special
You have another character beside you: your 'minion'. It knows 1 + Your Level Basic Spells from the Maneuvers, Divine, Eldritch and Arcane spell lists. It has Training in one Weapon Category and gets Training in another again at levels 3, 6 and 9. You share the same Actions between yourselves. Its main stat is Wisdom. Ex: If you used 0.5 Actions on your turn, your minion has 0.5 Actions left to use on its turn.

Choose One...

Celestial Follower
Passive
Your minion is a Celestial being, whose task is to protect, guide or follow you until its purpose is met. Your Celestial follower can be a humanoid or animal in appearance (it can't fly) and is sentient. Come up with a story about why you have a Celestial follower, and why your god allowed this or gave him or her to you. Your minion's max Health is always half of your max Health.
If it's animal-shaped and you want to use the rules for weapon attacks, just pretend the minion bites or scratches, but apply normal weapon rules.
True Necromancy
Passive
Your minion is a reanimated humanoid, whose soul is compelled to stay in this plane until its purpose is met. Come up with a story about why you have an Undead minion and why your god allowed this or gave him or her to you. Awe dice increases by 1 tier when healing yourself (or your minion). Your minion shares the same Health pool with you.

Level 4

Cleric Lich Ritual
Special
You spend 24 hours in a special place of power (this could be a temple or given to you by your DM). After it's finished, you become a Lich, and you are considered Undead. Apply the following: - You are Undead, thus immune to Charm, Fear and being put to Sleep - You no longer need to eat, drink or sleep. - You gain access to the Elemental and Eldritch Spell Lists. - You can no longer cast Basic Spells, but gain 2 extra slots for known Advanced Spells. Passive Basic Spells still apply. - The range of all your Spells is doubled. - Your Health decreases by 5, but your Defense, Spell DC and all Combat Checks increase by 1. You also gain the following abilities: - Phylactery - Dismorphment
Your Follower can still cast Basic Spells.
Life Echo
0 Actions
You put a Life Echo on an ally. Whenever YOU heal another Unit, the unit with the Life Echo on them is also healed for 50% of that amount. Lasts 1 minute.
If you put this on your True Necromancy follower, you essentially heal for 150% of the health.
Dazzling Essence
Passive
Your follower's first Spell or Attack every encounter attempts to also Daze the target (against a Might Check). Whenever you drop to (or below) 50% Health, this effect resets this encounter.
Phylactery
Special
When you become a Lich, you place your soul inside a Phylactery - a special object, dear to you. When you would reach 0 Health, instead of going Down, you can instantly teleport your body inside the Phylactery. A spellcaster can channel during a whole Long Rest to resummon you near the Phylactery with full Health, Spells, etc. If the Phylactery is destroyed, you must wait 1 year to make another one.
The Long Rest still applies to the channeler who revives you, and to you as well.
Dismorphment
Reaction or 0 Actions
Long Rest
When a Unit declares an attack or Spell on you, you can instantly teleport up to 6 meters away. That Unit can keep their action or change it. You can also instantly teleport using 0 Actions on your turn.
