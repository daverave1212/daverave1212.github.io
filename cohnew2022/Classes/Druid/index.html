Exhaust-Based Spellcasting

Druid Abilities are Exhaust-based.

As a Druid, you know a certain number of Advanced Abilities. You can cast each Advanced Ability you know ONCE, then it becomes unusable (Exhausted) until your next Long Rest.
All your Abilities Un-exhaust when you finish a Long Rest. Advanced Abilities are all Abilities from the Spell Lists listed as Advanced.

Changing Spells

You can change your known Spells (not Talents) when taking a Long Rest.
Talents can't generally be changed once picked; they are permenant decisions.All Druid-specific abilities are considered spells (unless stated otherwise).

Starting Abilities

Shapeshift
1 Action
2 times / Long Rest (see effect)
Pick an animal from the Animal Pets and Shapeshift Animals list and instantly transform into it. As soon as you Shapeshift, you can perform an Animal Attack. You gain that animal's Fortitude and Dexterity saves. Apply any other modifiers (except for base Stats) from that animal. You also gain its exact abilities. While Shapeshifted, you can do Animal Attack. While Shapeshifted, you can do any maneuvers you know. You can't cast spells. When you Shapeshift, your Health stays the same, unless the animal states it has a bonus/minus to Health. When Shapeshift ends, subtract back whatever health you added, or add back whatever health you subtracted. When you end Shapeshift, if your health would be 0 or less, it stays at 1. Shapeshift ends if you drop to 0 Health, and you go Unconscious. At first, you can't fly, even if your animal can. If your animal can fly, you have Proficiency in Jumping and you can glide (you don't take falling damage). From Level 3, you can use this 3 times per Long Rest. From Level 6, you can use this 4 times per Long Rest and you can fly. From Level 9, you can use this 5 times per Long Rest.
You can't make attacks with ranged weapons while Shapeshifted (obviously). You choose what items you keep on you when Shapeshifting, and those items are 'merged' into your new form. You can turn back into your humanoid form for 0 Actions (on your turn).

Druidcraft
Depends
You can create harmless sensory effects or tiny weather effects (6 meters range). You can massively speed up the growth of small plants (touch range). You can slightly soothe physical pains (with no combat effect) (touch range). You can attempt to communicate simple ideas with animals and plants (3 meters range). You have +3 on rolls for identifying plants. You can do these even while Shapeshifted.

Druidcraft

Druids have the ability to create small magical effects that affect plants, animals or just nature. You can use the Druidcraft Spell in roleplay scenarios to affect the environment and people.

Shapeshift

As a Druid, you can tranform into various animals, both in combat and outside of combat. The Shapeshift Spell provides an infinite amount of options to help out your party in any situation! You can choose any animal from the Animals list to Shapeshift into, as long as it's not Exotic or non-standard. Each Animal allows you to use its special Ability once per Long Rest. You are still only allowed to use that Ability once per Long Rest, even if you Shapeshift into that animal twice (the cooldown stays the same). Other Druid Talents might allow you to Shapeshift into more unusual beasts. Shapeshift is considered a Spell. {Separator} When in Shapeshift form, you always have access to the Animal Attack Ability, which is not considered a Spell. Animal Attack is, of course, impaired if you are Crippled.

Specialzations

The first time you choose a cleric domain (specialization), you must choose between two abilities. For example, for Battle Cleric, you have to choose either March Ahead or Piety. Choose wisely...

Coven of the Old Ways

You worship the sun, the moon and the stars, for only they can truly guide your path. Pagan Druids believe no one has the right to dictate someone else's spiritual path. As a Pagan Druid, you oppose fanaticism, opressors and stupidity. Pagan Druids usually worship no god, but they do acknowledge their power and they heavily respect nature and the earth. A Pagan Druid gains the power through not black, nor white magic. The suggested alignment for Pagan Druids is True Neutral.

Day Night Cycle
Passive
Instead of knowing one set of spells, as normal, you know two: one for the moon, and one for the sun. The Moon spells can only be used at night (from end of dusk until start of dawn). The Sun spells can only be used at day (from start of dawn until end of dusk). The Sun spell set and the Moon spell set can not contain any of the same spell.
So, in total, you know twice as many Basic and Advanced spells.

Coven of the Wild

You are heavily in touch with nature and with strange creatures, uncommon to the normal folk.

You start with...

Wild Shape
Passive
When you use Shapeshift, you can transform into a combination of 2 animals. You take the highest stat from both animals, and you have access to both of their abilities.
For example, you could transform into a combination of Bird of Prey and Bear, resulting in a kind of Owlbear, with a lot of health and a lot of Armor! Be creative! The animal size is either the larger or the smaller of the two - your choice. Even if you Shapeshift into 2 combinations of a same animal, the cooldown of that animal's Ability persists (e.g. you can't use Bear's Maul twice per Long Rest, no matter what animal combination you transform into).
